🌏 Earthquake

🟢 Before

  • Secure heavy furniture (cabinets, shelves)
  • Prepare emergency kit (water, flashlight, first aid)
  • Identify safe spots (under table, away from windows)
  • Practice "Drop, Cover, Hold"

🔴 During

  • DROP, COVER, HOLD ON
  • Stay away from glass and falling objects
  • Do NOT run outside while shaking
  • If outside → move to open area

🔵 After

  • Check yourself and others for injuries
  • Expect aftershocks
  • Avoid damaged buildings
  • Listen to official updates
🏢

Earthquake Safety Tips

🌪️ Typhoon

🟢 Before

  • Monitor weather updates
  • Secure roof, windows, and loose items
  • Prepare food, water, and power banks
  • Know evacuation routes

🔴 During

  • Stay indoors and away from windows
  • Turn off electricity if flooding risk
  • Do not go outside
  • Keep emergency kit nearby

🔵 After

  • Avoid flooded areas
  • Check for damages and hazards
  • Boil water before drinking
  • Follow government advisories
💨

Typhoon Safety Tips

🌊 Flood

🟢 Before

  • Elevate important items
  • Prepare emergency supplies
  • Know safe evacuation areas
  • Clear drainage around home

🔴 During

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Do NOT walk or drive through floodwater
  • Turn off electricity
  • Stay informed

🔵 After

  • Avoid contaminated water
  • Clean and disinfect your home
  • Watch for diseases
  • Follow safety advisories
💧

Flood Safety Tips

🔥 Fire

🟢 Before

  • Install smoke alarms
  • Prepare fire extinguisher
  • Plan escape routes
  • Avoid overloading electrical outlets

🔴 During

  • Stay low to avoid smoke
  • Use stairs, NOT elevators
  • Call emergency services
  • Get out immediately

🔵 After

  • Do not re-enter until safe
  • Check for injuries
  • Report damages
  • Seek assistance if needed
🚪
